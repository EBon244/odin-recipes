Milk Steak

Description

A dish created by Charlie Day in the hit show It's Always Sunny in Philadelphia. This is an elegant dish only served to the most distinguished customers of a restaurant.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Steak
  • Raw Jelly Beans

Steps

  1. Add 2 cups of milk to a pan and bring to a boil.
  2. Add in your favorite cut of steak into the pan.
  3. Cook until your desired doneness.
  4. Plate and add Raw Jelly Beans as a garnish.
