Krabby Patty

Description

"Made famous in the hit show Sponge-Bob Square-Pants. It is a staple to the Krusty Krab's success with its secret formula being often sought after."
A hamburger so good that not even King Neptune himself could produce a burger to match it's taste!

Ingredients:

  • Buns
  • Veggie Patty
  • Cheese
  • Onions
  • Pickle
  • Tomato
  • Lettuce
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard

Steps:

  1. Cook your veggie patty to your desired level of doneness.
  2. Next add your veggie patty to a bun
  3. Followed by your ketchup and mustard
  4. Add pickles
  5. Add extra onions
  6. Add lettuce
  7. Add cheese
  8. Add tomato
  9. Finally top it all off with your bun all in that order.
