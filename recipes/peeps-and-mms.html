Peeps and Chili

Description:

A food created by Chidi from the show the good place. It is best prepared with the tune of the "Coconut" song sung for each step.
It makes for the perfect comfort food while you are having an existential crisis after finding out your life has no meaning!

Ingredients:

  • Fresh pot of chili
  • Peeps Marshmallow Chicks
  • M&M's Candy

Steps:

  1. Start by making a fresh pot of chili
  2. Then you put the Peeps in the chili pot
  3. Finally you add your M&M's
